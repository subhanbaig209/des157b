Usability Results

Observations:

Observation 1: The game format was well-received by the observer, who found it engaging and enjoyable. The interactive nature of the scavenger hunt added an element of fun to the experience.

Observation 2: The observer recommended changing the colors to enhance the visual appeal of the game. Adjusting the color scheme could make the interface more visually appealing and create a better overall user experience.

Observation 3: The observer suggested increasing the text size to improve readability. Enlarging the text will make it easier for users to read the instructions, questions, and hints, ensuring a more accessible and user-friendly interface.

Plan for Updates:

Based on the feedback, here's a plan to update the capstone project:

Color Scheme: Evaluate the current color scheme and choose a new color palette that aligns with the theme of the game and enhances visual appeal. Consider using complementary or contrasting colors to create a visually engaging experience.

Text Size: Increase the size of the text throughout the game, including the instructions, questions, hints, and score display. Ensure that the text is easily readable across different devices and screen sizes.

Format of Pop-up Alert: Replace the current pop-up alert with a modal dialog for a more polished and user-friendly experience. The modal can provide a clearer and more visually appealing presentation of the questions and answers, enhancing the overall game interface.

By implementing these updates, you can improve the visual aesthetics, readability, and usability of the capstone project, enhancing the overall user experience and addressing the feedback received during testing.
